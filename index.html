E-Summit’26: Where Creativity Meets Capital
The Battlefield Where
Ideas Turn Into Enterprises

E-Summit IIT Patna is a flagship entrepreneurship conclave that brings together visionaries, builders, and risk-takers. Here, ideas are challenged, refined, and forged into ventures ready to face the real world.

Through intense competitions, high-impact talks, and hands-on workshops, E-Summit serves as the proving ground for the next generation of founders.

Compete

High-stakes startup pitch battles and hackathons where ideas fight for validation.

Learn

Scale
